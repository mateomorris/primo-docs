Introduction | Building | Managing | Publishing

Introduction

primo sites are developed and managed block-by-block (compared to file-by-file).When you're ready to publish your site, choose a hosting provider of your choice.

Creating your site

You can get up and running with your primo site in no time by picking one of the handful of Themes or starting from scratch. And since you get direct access to your site's source code, you can customize them to look and act any way you want. 

Managing your site

Easily manage your website from a simple interface that's fully focused on content - no design or code until you need it. That means that you can invite any collaborators, no matter their skill level, to collaborate on your site with you. 

Publishing your site

Unlike other site builders, primo lets you host your site on your hosting provider of choice. This means that you can publish it to a global Content Delivery Network for free using hosting providers like [Netlify](https://netlify.com/) and [Vercel](https://vercel.com/).
